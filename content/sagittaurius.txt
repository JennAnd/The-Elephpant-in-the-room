Sagittarius is ruled by Jupiter, the largest planet of the Solar system and the greatest
beneficent giant in Astrology. Their enthusiasm has no boundaries, and these people
possess a great sense of humor accompanied by intense curiosity.
People born with the Sun in Sagittarius are playful, humorous, and they will enjoy having
their relationships fun and carefree. It is important for them to learn how to express their
opinions in a tolerant and socially acceptable way.
A Sagittarius likes  freedom, travel, philosophy and being outdoors.
They dislikes clingy people, being constrained, off-the-wall theories and details.
Strengths: Generous, idealistic and great sense of humor.
Weaknesses: Promises more than can deliver, very impatient and will say anything no matter
how undiplomatic.
