An Aquarius see the world as a place full of possibilities and have the need to be spontaneous,
follow the moment and live it to the fullest in an attempt not to waste any time.
A visionary and someone ready for changes, an Aquarius is a known thinker and someone 
who will shake your world once they are in it. They feel best as a part of a certain community,
but might have trouble finding just the right place where they feel they belong. 
An Aquarius likes fun with friends, risky business, fighting for causes and intellectual conversations. 
They dislike limitations, broken promises, being lonely and dull or boring situations.
Strengths: Progressive, original, independent and humanitarian.
Weaknesses: Runs from emotional expression, temperamental, uncompromising and aloof.
