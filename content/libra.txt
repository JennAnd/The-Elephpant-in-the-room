Planet ruling the sign of Libra is Venus, making these people great lovers but also
fond of expensive, material things. 
They will be inspired by good books, insurmountable discussions and people who have
a lot to say. Libra is the sign of marriage, making its representatives open for traditional
pathways of love.
Once they start a romantic relationship, maintaining peace and harmony become
the most important thing and their primary goal.
A Libra likes harmony, gentleness, sharing with others and the outdoors.
They dislike violence, injustice, loudmouths and conformity.
Strengths: Cooperative, diplomatic, gracious, fair-minded and social.
Weaknesses: Indecisive, avoids confrontations, will carry a grudge and self-pity.
