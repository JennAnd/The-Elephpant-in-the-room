sentimental, Cancer can be one of the most challengiing Zodiac signs to get to know.
A Cancer likes art, homebased hobbies, relaxing near or in water, helping loved ones an a 
good meal with friends.
They dislike strangers, any criticism of mom and revealing of personal life.
Strengths: Tenacious, highly imaginative, loyal, emotional, sympathetic and persuasive.
