 A Capricorns influence makes these people practical and responsible, but also cold, distant and unforgiving,
prone to the feeling of guilt and turned to the past. Turned to their personal goals,
whatever they might be, Capricorns can lack compassion and emotion when relating to their loved
ones. Capricorn is a sign that represents time and responsibility, and its
representatives are traditional and often very serious by nature.
A Capricorn likes family, tradition, music, understated status and quality craftmanship.
They dislike almost everything at some point. 
Strengths: Responsible, diciplined, self-control and good managers.
Weaknesses: Know-it-all, unforgiving, condecending and expecting the worst.
